OUR RESPONSIBILITIES

How We Represent You

The Student Advisory Committee (SAC) is a student group established within each academic unit to promote communication and interaction among students, faculty, staff, and administrators. SACs are both a social and academic organization within their academic unit, and as such, they advance involvement opportunities, encourage participation in departmental activities, and work together with others to build campus community.

The Chairs of the committee represent your interests at the College of Fine Arts Student Council.

Dear School of Music Students, 

I am excited to announce my candidacy for re-election as the Undergraduate Chair of the Student Advisory Committee. I am running for another term on SAC because of my abiding passion for serving the educational institutions of which I am a part. 

I have a history of involvement in the School of Music, where I have found immense fulfilment in actively representing and serving the student body. My primary purpose is to ensure that the voices and desires of the students within the School of Music are heard. I want to foster a sense of community within the School of Music so we can grow with each other. I want to ensure that our resources in the School of Music are used appropriately to address student needs. I aim to be a proactive advocate for the School of Music students on the College of Fine Arts Student Council by actively participating in meetings and engaging with the responsibilities of this position. I believe it's crucial that the interests of our students are not only heard but also reflected in the actions of the Schools and Colleges of which we are a part; this involves working collaboratively with faculty, staff, and administrators to address concerns and propose impactful changes that benefit the School of Music community. 

As Undergraduate Chair for this past academic year, I have had the privilege of helping student organizations draft grants to the FAF Grants Committee to ensure they had a greater chance of receiving funding. I have worked closely with other members of SAC to make advancements in our administrative operations, including drafting a constitution that solidifies a documented process for how we operate and will help the future leaders of SAC have an understanding of the roles and responsibilities of the position and initiating the creation of a unified departmental email for the committee, so we can better serve you as we hear your concerns. Additionally, I have had the privilege of convening the Student Advisory Committee to discuss events that would be helpful to School of Music students, including grant writing workshops and more opportunities for School of Music students to perform with each other. 

I am excited to serve for another academic year so I can continue to advance the positive influence of the SAC on the School of Music. I am grateful for the opportunity I have had to serve you as your Undergraduate Chair this past academic year; thank you for considering my candidacy for another term.

FAQ

Problems Accessing Your Ballot?

To protect the the accuracy of the election results. The election ballot requires students to sign into the University's Active Directory to record their vote. Sometimes this can be difficult, so some instructions are here. If you have any problems, please reach out to SAC@music.utah.edu. 

 

Who Should Vote?

All students who have a declared program in the school of music should vote!
 

Why Should I Vote?

Voting in the SAC election gives you the opportunity to choose representatives who will voice your concerns and ideas within the School of Music. Your vote helps shape the direction and initiatives of the SAC, ensuring that student perspectives are considered in decision-making processes.
 

What Happens After the Election?

Once the voting period concludes, the ballots will be counted, and the candidates with the highest number of votes will be elected as representatives to the Student Advisory Council. Elected representatives will then begin their terms at the start of the 2024 â€“ 2025 Academic Year. 
 

When is the Deadline to Cast Votes?

The deadline to submit your vote will be on Friday, 12 April 2024 at 5:00 PM!

Results will be announced on 15 April 2024.
